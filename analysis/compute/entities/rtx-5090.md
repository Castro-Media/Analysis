## Description

A consumer desktop with one GeForce RTX 5090 is published by NVIDIA at over 3,352 trillion AI
operations per second.[^nvidia-rtx-5090]

## Scope

This entry converts the published 3352 AI TOPS figure to dense INT8-equivalent throughput using
the report normalization rules. Applying /2 from FP4 to INT8 and /2 to remove structured sparsity
yields 838 dense INT8 TOPS, or 8.38e+14 operations per second.[^nvidia-rtx-5090]

**Total compute:** 8.38e+14 dense INT8 operations per second.

## Implications

RTX 5090-class personal systems push individual-accessible AI throughput into territory previously
reserved for small server deployments while preserving comparability on the dense INT8 scale.
