## Description

A consumer desktop with one GeForce RTX 5090 is published by NVIDIA at over 3,352 trillion AI
operations per second.[^nvidia-rtx-5090]

## Scope

This entry records the published 3352 AI TOPS figure as 3.352e+15 operations per second in dataset
notation.[^nvidia-rtx-5090]
The number is an official vendor AI-throughput metric and may not map exactly to a conservative
strict dense INT8-only value.

**Total compute:** 3.352e+15 operations per second (vendor AI TOPS based).

RTX 5090-class personal systems push individual-accessible AI throughput into territory previously
reserved for small server deployments. Comparisons against dense INT8-only rows should keep vendor
AI TOPS methodology differences explicit.
